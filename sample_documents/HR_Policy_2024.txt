# GlobalTech Innovations - HR Policy 2024

## 1. Paid Time Off (PTO)
All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of Paid Time Off (PTO) per calendar year. PTO accrues at a rate of 1.66 days per month. Up to 5 unused PTO days can be rolled over to the next calendar year. Any additional unused days will be forfeited.

## 2. Remote Work Policy
Employees are allowed to work remotely up to 3 days per week. Core office days are Tuesday and Thursday. When working remotely, employees are expected to be online and available during core business hours (10:00 AM to 3:00 PM local time).

## 3. Sick Leave
Employees are provided with 10 days of paid sick leave annually. This can be used for personal illness, medical appointments, or to care for an immediate family member. Sick leave does not carry over to the next year.

## 4. Hardware and Equipment
The company provides a standard hardware package (laptop, monitor, keyboard, mouse) to all employees. A one-time stipend of $500 is provided to assist with setting up a home office. Equipment upgrades can be requested every 3 years.

## 5. Performance Reviews
Performance reviews are conducted bi-annually, in June and December. Raises and promotions are typically evaluated during the December review cycle.
